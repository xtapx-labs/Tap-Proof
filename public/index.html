How It Works

The Asset Literacy Moment in three steps

Tap the product

Hold your phone near the NFC tag. The tag's secure chip powers up, increments its counter, and generates a fresh cryptographic signature that has never existed before.

Automatic verification

Your phone opens a URL containing the encrypted data. Our server decrypts it, validates the signature, checks the counter for anomalies, and builds the Digital Title Deed.

Think. Decide. Confidence.

You see the verdict — authentic, counterfeit, or suspicious — with the product's full provenance chain. Make your decision based on mathematically verified information, not trust.
